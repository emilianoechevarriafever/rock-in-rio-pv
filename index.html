Description

Since its debut in 2004, Rock in Rio Lisboa has transformed the city into a global music stage. The 2026 edition promises to elevate the experience even further, with a powerful international lineup, a new audiovisual show on the Main Stage, a brand-new Halftime Show in the clearing, additional fireworks, and new interactive attractions for all ages.

Join the Rock in Rio community and live an unforgettable celebration of music, diversity, and culture.

About the venue

Parque Tejo
Tejo Park, located in eastern Lisbon by the river, is a versatile spot for big outdoor events. In 2026, it will once again host Rock in Rio Lisbon, welcoming thousands of fans for an unforgettable festival experience.
